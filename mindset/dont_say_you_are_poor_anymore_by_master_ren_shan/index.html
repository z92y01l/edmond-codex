Don't Say You're Poor Anymore by Master Ren Shan

Original​

  • 停止負面口頭禪 (Stop Negative Self-Talk)：法師勸導大家，以後不要再說自己**「窮了」或「沒錢花」**了。
  • 語言創造現實 (Words Create Reality)：他解釋，這些口頭習慣一旦養成，會導致**「心想事成」**。如果一個人不斷說自己沒錢花，就會越來越沒錢；如果說自己好苦，以後就會越來越苦。
  • 面對困境的態度 (Attitude Towards Hardship)：即使遇到困難的人生際遇，也不要皺眉頭或說自己很窮、很難過。應該將其視為**「人生的小菜一碟」**，很快就會過去。
  • 最大的財富是自己 (You Are Your Greatest Wealth)：法師強調，縱然外在一切都沒有，「只要還有一口氣在，自己就是最大的財富」。
  • 痛苦的來源 (The Source of Suffering)：一個人感到貧窮和痛苦，不是因為真的缺乏，而是因為自己內心產生了**「貧窮的感覺」**，覺得自己真的缺少了很多東西。

English Translation (Gemini)​

  • Avoid Negative Language: The Master advises people to stop saying they are poor or have no money.

  • Words Create Reality: He explains that once these negative verbal habits are formed, they lead to "what the heart desires will come true" (心想事成). If someone constantly says, "I have no money, I have no money," they will find themselves mysteriously having less and less money. Similarly, if they constantly say, "I am suffering," their life will become increasingly miserable.

  • Perspective on Hardship: When faced with severe hardship, one should not frown or say they are sad or poor. Instead, view the problem as "just a small side dish in life" (人生的小菜一碟) that will quickly pass.

  • Self as the Greatest Wealth: The ultimate treasure is your own existence. Even if you outwardly possess nothing, "as long as you still have a breath, you are the greatest wealth".

  • The Root of Suffering: Poverty only feels miserable because a person genuinely believes and feels that they are lacking many things. If a person does not have the feeling of poverty, they will not suffer.
